Seeking to overthrow Emperor <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maximinus_Thrax" title="Maximinus Thrax">Maximinus Thrax</a>, he died in battle outside <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carthage" title="Carthage">Carthage</a>. Since he died before his father, Gordian II had the shortest reign of any Roman Emperor in the whole of the Empire's history, at 21 days.
